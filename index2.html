About me

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the National University of Singapore, working under the supervision of Gim Hee Lee. Previously, I obtained my Bachelors of Engineering (Computer Engineering) from National University of Singapore in 2011. After graduating, I worked several years in DSO National Laboratories, which is Singapore's Defence R&D organization. During that time I also obtained a Computer Science Masters from Georgia Institute of Technology under their OMSCS program. My research interest is in Computer Vision, particularly in the area of Deep Learning and Point Cloud processing. To keep my sanity, my personal interests include consumer technology and photography.

I can be contacted at zyew3 at comp.nus.edu.sg
